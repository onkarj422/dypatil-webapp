About College

Dr. D.Y. Patil Arts, Commerce and Science College is a distinguished degree College affiliated to the University of Pune. It is situated away from din and bustle of the metropolitan Pune, just a few yards off the Bombay-Pune Highway behind H.D.F.C. Colony, Shaunagar, Chinchwad, Pune.

In addition to the senior section, the college has junior section, imparting education to F.Y.J.C. and S.Y.J.C. in Commerce, Science and Bifocal(Computer Science).

The college has established in 2011 by S.M. Dr. D.Y. Patil Shikshan Sanstha with the view of providing quality education in various courses of studies leading to B.Com, B.Sc, Computer Science, B.C.A. affiliated to university of Pune.

The college has pledged to adopt the tradition of rigorous training, individual attention and periodical tested which are unanimously acknowledged as a key to excellent performance yielding positive and encouraging results as a result the students maintain commendable results in university exams and return home with laurels of victor won in various competitions and tournaments.
